## How It Was Made
Hook, Line and Sinker was built entirely in **Unity 6** using **C#**.
The world uses assets from some incredible free packs:
- The **goblin character** comes from the [Sunnyside World Asset Pack](https://danieldiggle.itch.io/sunnyside) — a beautifully detailed top-down RPG pack with animations for everything from idle to casting to reeling to catching
- The **fish player** is the adorable clownfish from [AshenThrone's Fishie Sprite pack](https://ashenthrone.itch.io/fishie-sprite) — featuring 6-directional swim animations, ouch reactions and turning frames
- The **underwater enemies** — octopus, jellyfish, shark, anglerfish and eel — come from [Free Game Assets](https://free-game-assets.itch.io/octopus-jellyfish-shark-and-turtle-free-sprite-pixel-art)
- The **underwater parallax background** is from [Ansimuz's Underwater Fantasy pack](https://ansimuz.itch.io/underwater-fantasy-pixel-art-environment)
- The **world tiles, UI and dock** are built from the [Sprout Lands UI](https://cupnooble.itch.io/sprout-lands-ui-pack) and [Sunnyside asset packs sprite](https://cupnooble.itch.io/sprout-lands-asset-pack)
- The **music** is from [Cozy Tunes Pro](https://pizzadoggy.itch.io/cozy-tunes) — gentle ambient tracks that shift depending on whether you're fishing, roaming or underwater
- **Sound effects** come from the JDSherbert Wooden UI SFX Pack and custom sourced fishing sounds

The bubble transition between scenes is taken directly from a classic Spongebob-style wipe, converted to a sprite sheet and animated in engine.
- Fish sprite by [AshenThrone](https://ashenthrone.itch.io/fishie-sprite) — licensed under CC BY-NC-ND 4.0
- Enemy sprites by [Free Game Assets](https://free-game-assets.itch.io)
- Underwater environment by [Ansimuz](https://ansimuz.itch.io)
- Character and world art from [Sunnyside World Asset Pack](https://danieldiggle.itch.io/sunnyside) and [Sprout Lands UI Pack](https://cupnooble.itch.io/sprout-lands-ui-pack)
- Music from [Cozy Tunes Pro](https://pizzadoggy.itch.io/cozy-tunes)

*A goblin. A dock. A fish that might be you.*
